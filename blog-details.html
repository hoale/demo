Staff Bio – Angel Chan

October 21, 2013
By Joyride Info

 

 

Hometown?

San Francisco, CA

Alma Mater, Degree?

UC Santa Barbara, Communication and Psychology (Go Gauchos!)

How did you get into coffee?

I was a barista at a local coffee shop (Nicoletti’s) during college. It made me enjoy coffee and fall in love with the coffee experience that much more when I could share it with other coffee enthusiasts. Studying abroad in Italy (and drinking way too many cups of cappuccino) also opened my eyes to the complexity and intricacy of coffee preparation.

What is your first memory of coffee?

Going to get coffee with my mom and dad every Saturday morning

What do you do at Joyride?

My job is to make sure our customers are happy and always caffeinated with the best coffee. I help deal with their orders, and answer any questions about our coffees.

Favorite coffee of the year?

Hands down, Stumptown’s Valle de Los Santos. On hot days, cold brew is perfect.

What do you order when you go to a bar?

If not at a coffee bar, I like a good margarita, mojito or Belgian beer.

Favorite Book?

The Giving Tree (Yes, the children book)
