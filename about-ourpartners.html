Our Partners

Great people doing great things.

We have been lucky enough to meet some amazing people doing some amazing things. Here are some of our partners and projects we sponsor.

Walkabout NYC

We love Walkabout; it's a wonderful event organized by long-time Joyride customer, Harvest. This year we'll not just be attending, but also lending our java-support as "Awesome Sponsors."

WalkaboutNYC is a behind-the-scenes look at the people and places fueling our creative, technological, and entrepreneurial culture in New York City. Our events invite the world into the workspaces of NYC's innovative companies to see how they work.

 

The Coalition for Queens

Joyride is a proud sponsor of the Coalition for Queens. Their mission is to foster a tech entrepreneurial ecosystem in the borough of Queens through education, events, and advocacy. They also inform the Queens and broader communities about the tech industry and the growing opportunities it presents.

 

Monster Savings

Monster Savings is a locally owned small business that provides groceries and pantry items for significantly cheaper than Fresh Direct. They serve both businesses and residential customers in Manhattan with brand-name products, the lowest prices, and great customer service. They also sell beer!

 

Barrel

Barrel is a digital agency located in SoHo. They design websites, custom apps, e-commerce stores, and marketing campaigns. They also drink our brew and designed our website.

 

HackNY

Joyride is proud to sponsor HackNY. Co-organized by faculty from NYU and Columbia, and with a board of advisors which includes educators, technologists, and entrepreneurs, HackNY organizes the summer Fellows program and student 'hackathons' during the school year in order to create and empower a community of student-technologists.

 

Projective Space

Joyride is happy to sponsor Projective Space. Projective Space is home to a carefully curated mix of NYC-based creative entrepreneurial ventures. They have multiple locations in Manhattan.

 

Open Tech NYC 2013

Joyride is proud to sponsor the Open Tech NYC 2013 Conference. The conference covers topics from locally grown open source projects to big enterprises and new models developed on an open source platform.
