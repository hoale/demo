Service

We do everything to make your life easier and your coffee tastier


Our equipment leasing is full service. We make our machines easy to use, and effortless to maintain. To make sure they stay that way we take some extraordinary steps:


▸ Monthly airpot swap and coffee area cleanup. We deep clean all your equipment so your coffee doesn't start to taste like an old sock.
▸ Free equipment maintenance. Have a question? Call us and you will get a person, every time. If we can't walk you through your issue over the phone, our tech will be at your office within 48 hours to fix the issue and get you brewing.
▸ Every new coffee you order comes with a laminated tag, that velcros to the airpots so everybody knows what they are drinking.
▸ All equipment comes with simple brew instructions so your coffee is always delicious.
▸ Have tons of meetings? We can lease extra airpots for only $5/mon. We'll swap these monthly to keep them fresh.


We are dedicated to keeping our equipment meticulously maintained, and our service attentive. We want you to love your machine, and the coffee it makes. It takes more effort on our part to do things this way, but to us, it's worth it.
