Our Team

We Love Coffee


Joyride is owned and operated by three brothers who drink far too much coffee. Originally, they ran a mobile café dishing Stumptown coffee on the streets of Manhattan. Customers often lamented the quality of their own office coffee, and an idea was born: providing amazing office coffee with café-quality equipment and active, engaged service. Now, Joyride delivers freshly roasted coffee from amazing roasters to offices across the tri-state region.


Since the days of the truck, Joyride has grown to include a great group of people.
