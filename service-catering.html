Catering

Artisanal Coffee Catering

In addition to our office distribution, we do premium quality coffee catering. We offer two different ways to get amazing coffee at your event:

1. Drop Off Service:

We'll come and deliver freshly brewed coffee to you. The coffee is brought in disposable cardboard containers with description cards for the coffee included. We can drop off enough coffee for a two hundred, or a thousand person, event. Enjoy the coffee and toss the boxes when you're done. Simple!


2. Full Service Brew Bar:

Joyride offers a full service brew bar complete with several hand-brew techniques as well as a professional barista to work their magic. As far as coffee catering, this is unique. If you are looking for a way to turn an event into a truly gourmet endeavor, a brew bar is simply perfect. Our baristas are friendly and love to talk about their craft and coffee in general. Not only delicious, a brew bar can be an informative event in itself.

Please call us at (917) 670-3314 or use the contact us page for pricing and scheduling.

NOTE: There is a 200 person minimum for all events. Prices start at $1000.
